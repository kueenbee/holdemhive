New Player Policies

As of 7/2025

Definition: A New Player is anyone who has (1) been phone screened by a Hive host and (2) played less than 3 live games in the league. After playing 3 live games you become a league member!

League Members are players who (1) have played in 3 or more live games or (2) are an immediate famly member of a league member or (3) are friends or family of the Hive hosts or (4) played in Jim's Poker Table League.

The Hive will seat only one New Player per live (@ the Hive) game, on a first-come-first-serve basis.

To reserve the single "New Player" seat, you must send (Venmo) the Hive your $30 buy-in for the upcoming live game after 6 PM the Sunday before (reservations for future games are not accepted).

If you are not the first person to reserve the New Player seat, your buy-in will be refunded immediately.


Refund Policy for Cancellation of "New Player" Seat Reservation

If you don't formally cancel your New Player seat reservation and don't come to the game by the end of the first break, you forfeit your buy-in and will only be allowed to play in a future game if no other New Player wants to play the week you wish to play.

If you cancel your New Player seat reservation before the day of the tournament you will get an immediate refund.

If you cancel your New Player seat reservation on the day of the event you will get a refund if and only if

(1) another new player fills the seat OR

(2) there are no other new players who want to play (assume this will be the case)

If, due to late cancellation, no interested new player can fill the seat, your non-refunded buy-in will go into the pot for the game in question.


Note: New Players who have played at least one live game are welcome to play in any online games (PokerStars) without having to pre-reserve a seat.

The League reserves the right to make exceptions to this protocol at its sole discretion but will not consider requests to do so.
