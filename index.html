Rochester NY Poker - A Premier No Limit Hold 'Em League

    Hold ’Em Hive is a private, live poker league in Rochester, NY offering weekly No Limit Hold ’Em games for local players.

    Started in 2023 and Currently in Season 13 (January 2026)

  • Weekly games on Thursdays at 6:45 PM (4-5 hour game)
  • $30/game buy in (no rake/tip/fee!), 1600 chips (160 BB) starting stack, $30 rebuys 1300 chips (all dollars to the pot)
  • 8 game seasons (play 1, play all 8, as convenient for you), 4 seasons per year, season championships
  • 6-MAX Tournament of Champions
